The title Inncircles Website

A Digital Innovation

During my tenure at Inncircles, I was an integral part of the Progress team, contributing to the development of a website aimed at minimizing paperwork within the construction domain. Joining the team in March 2021, I embarked on a dynamic journey, starting with the creation of front-end sites tailored to project requirements. As I progressed, my role expanded into the realms of Node.js and MongoDB. One significant accomplishment was the development of a pivotal feature known as user management. This feature allowed us to efficiently handle all users associated with the project, facilitating tasks such as addition or removal from specific projects, assignment of permissions for accessing specific components, and the creation of new users by administrators. This experience not only enhanced my proficiency in Angular and Node.js but also broadened my understanding of complex project dynamics

Throughout this process, I collaborated with a diverse and talented team, fostering a collaborative environment that encouraged continuous learning. My role required not only technical expertise but also effective communication and problem-solving skills, both of which I honed during this challenging yet rewarding project.
