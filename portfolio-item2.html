The title Discussion Board

Shared Platform.

The Discussion Board project, a collaborative effort undertaken during my undergraduate studies, is a MERN stack-based website designed to address the needs of students who may hesitate to ask questions in a traditional classroom setting. Functioning as a discussion forum, students create accounts on the platform, allowing them to post questions. Any registered user, including fellow students and faculty members, can then respond to these queries. My primary focus in this group project was on the backend development using Node.js and MongoDB. I was responsible for crafting APIs and implementing various functionalities within the Node.js environment. The decision to use MongoDB as our database system was driven by its NoSQL structure, providing ease of data querying and retrieval. This project provided me with a comprehensive understanding of backend development, honing my skills in Node.js and deepening my insights into the storage mechanisms employed by MongoDB

Through this endeavor, I not only contributed to the creation of a valuable tool for students but also expanded my knowledge of MERN stack technologies, enhancing my ability to develop robust and functional backend systems for web applications. This project became a cornerstone in my academic journey, fostering a passion for web development and laying the groundwork for further exploration in the field.
